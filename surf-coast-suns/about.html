Our Story

More than a club —
a community

Surf Coast Cricket Club is a newly established, community-minded and inclusive club in the growing area of North Torquay. Based at the Banyul-Warri Fields Sporting Precinct on Merrijig Drive, we compete in the GCA.

Formed through the merger of the Surf Coast Stingrays and the Surf Coast Suns Football Netball Club, we field 4 senior men’s sides and a growing junior program covering U/11 through U/17. We acknowledge the Wadawurrung People as the Traditional Custodians of the land on which we play.

Our brand new 4-lane cricket nets at Banyul-Warri Fields — funded by a $520,000 community investment — reflect the ambition and momentum behind everything we do.

What We Stand For

Our Values

Everything we do on and off the field is guided by the principles that define Surf Coast Cricket Club.

Community First

We’re built on the North Torquay community. Families, friends, and locals are the backbone of everything we do.

Inclusive Cricket

Cricket is for everyone. We welcome all skill levels, ages, and backgrounds — from first-timers to seasoned players.

Competitive Spirit

We play to win but never at the expense of respect. Our teams compete hard in the GCA with sportsmanship at heart.
